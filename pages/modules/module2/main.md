## About this module 

This module introduces the key steps for developing a simple and practical Node data management strategy (NDMS). Each chapter covers one part of the process and includes short exercises and examples to help you apply the ideas to your own Node.

## Chapters 

**1. Why a Node data management strategy**

A short explanation of why an NDMS is useful and how it supports coordination, planning and communication within your Node

**3. What to include in a NDMS**

**4. How to begin developing a NDMS**

Practical first steps to start the strategy work, build momentum and connect existing materials and activities

**5. Planning and sustaining a NDMS** 

Guidance on turning strategy into action through planning, budgeting and long term sustainability
